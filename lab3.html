Chou Tzuyu

Chou Tzu-yu (Chinese: 周子瑜; pinyin: Zhōu Zǐyú), known mononymously as Tzuyu (Hangul: 쯔위), was born on June 14, 1999 (age 17) in Tainan, Taiwan.

Im Nayeon

Im Na-yeon (Hangul: 임나연) was born on September 22, 1995 (age 21) in Seoul, South Korea

Kim Dahyun

Kim Da-hyun (Hangul: 김다현) was born on May 28, 1998 (age 18) in Seongnam, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea.

Minatozaki Sana

Sana Minatozaki (湊崎 紗夏 Minatozaki Sana?), known mononymously as Sana (Hangul: 사나), was born on December 29, 1996 (age 20) in Tennōji-ku, Osaka, Japan.

Myoui Mina

Mina Myoui (名井 南 Myōi Mina?), known mononymously as Mina (Hangul: 미나), was born on March 24, 1997 (age 19) in San Antonio, Texas, and moved to Kobe, Japan, when she was a toddler.

Son Chaeyoung

Son Chae-young (Hangul: 손채영) was born on April 23, 1999 (age 17) in Seoul, South Korea.

Hirai Momo

Momo Hirai (平井 もも Hirai Momo?), known mononymously as Momo (Hangul: 모모), was born on November 9, 1996 (age 20) in Kyōtanabe, Kyoto, Japan.[60] She is the main dancer of Twice.

Yoo Jeongyeon

Yoo Jeong-yeon (Hangul: 유정연) was born as Yoo Kyung-wan (유경완) on November 1, 1996 (age 20) in Suwon, South Korea. She is the younger sister of actress Gong Seung-yeon. As a child, she was often teased because Kyung-wan sounded like a boy's name. She legally changed her name to Jeong-yeon when she was in the third grade.

Park Ji-hyo

Park Ji-hyo (Hangul: 박지효) was born as Park Ji-soo (Hangul: 박지수) on February 1, 1997 (age 20) in Guri, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea. She is the leader and main vocalist of the group.
